Our Process

In the following page we will see the process of making a shirt.

Our Process In Steps

  1. Material Required:

    • 1. A Heat Press

    • 2. A Silhousette (cutter) Machine.

    • 3. A Picture Printer

    • 4. A computer with a silhousette software

    • 5. A variety of different colord vinyl.

    • 6. Design of the logo on computer.


    • Step by Step Process:

    • Step 1: Put colored vinyl In the Silhousette (Cutter) Machine.

    • Step 2: Pull vinyl when machine is done with it.

    • Step 3: Put The Design On The Shirt.

    • Step 5: Press The Heat Press Machine.

    • Step 6: Give it about 10-15 Seconds.

    • Step 7: Remove The Plastic Paper.


Your Shirt Is Ready!!!
