Our Process

In the following page we will see the process of making a shirt.

Our Process In Steps

  1. Material Required:
    • A Heat Press
    • A Silhousette (Cutter) Machine.
    • A Picture Printer
    • A Computer With Silhousette Software
    • A Variety of different colors vinyl.
  2. Design The Logo On Computer.
  3. Put Colored Vinyl In The Silhousette (Cutter) Machine.
    • Pill The Vinyl When Machine Is Done With It.
  4. Put The Design On The Shirt.
    • Press The Heat Press Machine.
    • Give it about 10-15 Seconds.
    • Remove The Plastic Paper.

Your Shirt Is Ready!!!
